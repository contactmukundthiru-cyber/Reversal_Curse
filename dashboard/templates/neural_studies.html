EEG Components of Interest
P300 (300-500ms)

Context updating component. Expected to be larger for reversals, indicating increased updating demand.

N400 (350-500ms)

Semantic processing component. Enhanced N400 indicates semantic conflict during reversal processing.

Frontal Theta (4-8Hz)

Cognitive control oscillations. Increased theta power expected during effortful knowledge restructuring.
