Key Research Questions
Medical Domain
  • How do physicians communicate guideline changes to patients?
  • Does the reversal curse affect shared decision-making?
  • What interventions improve post-reversal patient education?
  • How does guideline reversal duration affect curse magnitude?
Climate Domain
  • How do scientists communicate updated projections?
  • Does updating projections undermine public trust?
  • What communication strategies reduce confusion?
  • How does projection direction (worse vs. better) affect the curse?
Study Design Framework
Phase 1: Baseline

Measure existing knowledge and beliefs about the domain topic before presenting the reversal.

Phase 2: Reversal

Present the knowledge reversal (guideline change or projection update) with varying communication strategies.

Phase 3: Assessment

Measure confusion, trust, behavioral intent, and knowledge retention at multiple follow-up points.
